<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> And in the seventh month, on the first day of the month, ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work: it is a day of blowing the trumpets unto you.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> And ye shall offer a burnt offering for a sweet savour unto the LORD; one young bullock, one ram, and seven lambs of the first year without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> And their meat offering shall be of flour mingled with oil, three tenth deals for a bullock, and two tenth deals for a ram,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And one tenth deal for one lamb, throughout the seven lambs:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And one kid of the goats for a sin offering, to make an atonement for you:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Beside the burnt offering of the month, and his meat offering, and the daily burnt offering, and his meat offering, and their drink offerings, according unto their manner, for a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And ye shall have on the tenth day of this seventh month an holy convocation; and ye shall afflict your souls: ye shall not do any work therein:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> But ye shall offer a burnt offering unto the LORD for a sweet savour; one young bullock, one ram, and seven lambs of the first year; they shall be unto you without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And their meat offering shall be of flour mingled with oil, three tenth deals to a bullock, and two tenth deals to one ram,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> A several tenth deal for one lamb, throughout the seven lambs:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> One kid of the goats for a sin offering; beside the sin offering of atonement, and the continual burnt offering, and the meat offering of it, and their drink offerings.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work, and ye shall keep a feast unto the LORD seven days:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And ye shall offer a burnt offering, a sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD; thirteen young bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year; they shall be without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And their meat offering shall be of flour mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto every bullock of the thirteen bullocks, two tenth deals to each ram of the two rams,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And a several tenth deal to each lamb of the fourteen lambs:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And one kid of the goats for a sin offering; beside the continual burnt offering, his meat offering, and his drink offering.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And on the second day ye shall offer twelve young bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs of the first year without spot:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And one kid of the goats for a sin offering; beside the continual burnt offering, and the meat offering thereof, and their drink offerings.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs of the first year without blemish;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And one goat for a sin offering; beside the continual burnt offering, and his meat offering, and his drink offering.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> Their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> And one kid of the goats for a sin offering; beside the continual burnt offering, his meat offering, and his drink offering.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without spot:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> And their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> And one goat for a sin offering; beside the continual burnt offering, and his meat offering, and his drink offering.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> And their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And on the seventh day seven bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without blemish:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> Their meat offering and their drink offerings for the bullock, for the ram, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> These things ye shall do unto the LORD in your set feasts, beside your vows, and your freewill offerings, for your burnt offerings, and for your meat offerings, and for your drink offerings, and for your peace offerings.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> And Moses told the children of Israel according to all that the LORD commanded Moses.
