<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> His foundation is in the holy mountains.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> The LORD loveth the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this man was born there.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> The LORD shall count, when he writeth up the people, that this man was born there. Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> As well the singers as the players on instruments shall be there: all my springs are in thee.
