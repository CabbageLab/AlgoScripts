<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> In my distress I cried unto the LORD, and he heard me.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar!

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war.
