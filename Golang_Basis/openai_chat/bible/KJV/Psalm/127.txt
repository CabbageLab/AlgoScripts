<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Except the LORD build the house, they labour in vain that build it: except the LORD keep the city, the watchman waketh but in vain.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> It is vain for you to rise up early, to sit up late, to eat the bread of sorrows: for so he giveth his beloved sleep.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Lo, children are an heritage of the LORD: and the fruit of the womb is his reward.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> As arrows are in the hand of a mighty man; so are children of the youth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Happy is the man that hath his quiver full of them: they shall not be ashamed, but they shall speak with the enemies in the gate.
