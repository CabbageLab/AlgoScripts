<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> To him that made great lights: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> The sun to rule by day: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> The moon and stars to rule by night: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> To him that smote Egypt in their firstborn: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And brought out Israel from among them: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And made Israel to pass through the midst of it: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> But overthrew Pharaoh and his host in the Red sea: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> To him which led his people through the wilderness: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> To him which smote great kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And slew famous kings: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> Sihon king of the Amorites: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And Og the king of Bashan: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And gave their land for an heritage: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> Even an heritage unto Israel his servant: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> Who remembered us in our low estate: for his mercy endureth for ever:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> Who giveth food to all flesh: for his mercy endureth for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> O give thanks unto the God of heaven: for his mercy endureth for ever.
