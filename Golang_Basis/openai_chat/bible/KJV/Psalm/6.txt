<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> O LORD, rebuke me not in thine anger, neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Have mercy upon me, O LORD; for I am weak: O LORD, heal me; for my bones are vexed.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> My soul is also sore vexed: but thou, O LORD, how long?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Return, O LORD, deliver my soul: oh save me for thy mercies' sake.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> For in death there is no remembrance of thee: in the grave who shall give thee thanks?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> I am weary with my groaning; all the night make I my bed to swim; I water my couch with my tears.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> Mine eye is consumed because of grief; it waxeth old because of all mine enemies.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; for the LORD hath heard the voice of my weeping.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> The LORD hath heard my supplication; the LORD will receive my prayer.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> Let all mine enemies be ashamed and sore vexed: let them return and be ashamed suddenly.
