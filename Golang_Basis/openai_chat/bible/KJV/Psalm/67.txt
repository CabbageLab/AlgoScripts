<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> God be merciful unto us, and bless us; and cause his face to shine upon us; Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Then shall the earth yield her increase; and God, even our own God, shall bless us.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
