<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.
