<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> How long wilt thou forget me, O LORD? for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy face from me?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Consider and hear me, O LORD my God: lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> But I have trusted in thy mercy; my heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> I will sing unto the LORD, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
