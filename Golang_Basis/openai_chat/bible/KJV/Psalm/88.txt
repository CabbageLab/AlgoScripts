<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> O LORD God of my salvation, I have cried day and night before thee:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Let my prayer come before thee: incline thine ear unto my cry;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> For my soul is full of troubles: and my life draweth nigh unto the grave.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> I am counted with them that go down into the pit: I am as a man that hath no strength:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Free among the dead, like the slain that lie in the grave, whom thou rememberest no more: and they are cut off from thy hand.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Thou hast laid me in the lowest pit, in darkness, in the deeps.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> Thy wrath lieth hard upon me, and thou hast afflicted me with all thy waves. Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> Thou hast put away mine acquaintance far from me; thou hast made me an abomination unto them: I am shut up, and I cannot come forth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> Mine eye mourneth by reason of affliction: LORD, I have called daily upon thee, I have stretched out my hands unto thee.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> Wilt thou shew wonders to the dead? shall the dead arise and praise thee? Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> Shall thy lovingkindness be declared in the grave? or thy faithfulness in destruction?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Shall thy wonders be known in the dark? and thy righteousness in the land of forgetfulness?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> But unto thee have I cried, O LORD; and in the morning shall my prayer prevent thee.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> LORD, why castest thou off my soul? why hidest thou thy face from me?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up: while I suffer thy terrors I am distracted.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> Thy fierce wrath goeth over me; thy terrors have cut me off.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> They came round about me daily like water; they compassed me about together.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Lover and friend hast thou put far from me, and mine acquaintance into darkness.
