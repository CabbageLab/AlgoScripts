<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of thy tender mercies blot out my transgressions.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my sin is ever before me.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> Purge me with hyssop, and I shall be clean: wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> Hide thy face from my sins, and blot out all mine iniquities.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> Cast me not away from thy presence; and take not thy holy spirit from me.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Restore unto me the joy of thy salvation; and uphold me with thy free spirit.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and sinners shall be converted unto thee.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my salvation: and my tongue shall sing aloud of thy righteousness.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> For thou desirest not sacrifice; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt offering.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Do good in thy good pleasure unto Zion: build thou the walls of Jerusalem.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices of righteousness, with burnt offering and whole burnt offering: then shall they offer bullocks upon thine altar.
