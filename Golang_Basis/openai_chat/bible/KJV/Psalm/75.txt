<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, unto thee do we give thanks: for that thy name is near thy wondrous works declare.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> When I shall receive the congregation I will judge uprightly.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the horn:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Lift not up your horn on high: speak not with a stiff neck.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> For promotion cometh neither from the east, nor from the west, nor from the south.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> But God is the judge: he putteth down one, and setteth up another.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> For in the hand of the LORD there is a cup, and the wine is red; it is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same: but the dregs thereof, all the wicked of the earth shall wring them out, and drink them.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off; but the horns of the righteous shall be exalted.
