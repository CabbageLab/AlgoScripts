<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the LORD glory and strength.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Give unto the LORD the glory due unto his name; worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD is upon the waters: the God of glory thundereth: the LORD is upon many waters.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD is powerful; the voice of the LORD is full of majesty.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD breaketh the cedars; yea, the LORD breaketh the cedars of Lebanon.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> He maketh them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young unicorn.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD divideth the flames of fire.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD shaketh the wilderness; the LORD shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> The voice of the LORD maketh the hinds to calve, and discovereth the forests: and in his temple doth every one speak of his glory.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> The LORD sitteth upon the flood; yea, the LORD sitteth King for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> The LORD will give strength unto his people; the LORD will bless his people with peace.
