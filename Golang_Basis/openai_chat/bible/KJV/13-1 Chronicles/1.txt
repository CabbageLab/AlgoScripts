<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Adam, Sheth, Enosh,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name was Joktan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> Eber, Peleg, Reu,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> Serug, Nahor, Terah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> Abram; the same is Abraham.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> These are their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of Keturah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> The sons of Esau; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>38</bmstyle> And the sons of Seir; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan's sister.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>41</bmstyle> The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>42</bmstyle> The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>43</bmstyle> Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city was Dinhabah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>44</bmstyle> And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>45</bmstyle> And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>46</bmstyle> And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>47</bmstyle> And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>48</bmstyle> And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>49</bmstyle> And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>50</bmstyle> And when Baalhanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife's name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>51</bmstyle> Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>52</bmstyle> Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>53</bmstyle> Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>54</bmstyle> Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These are the dukes of Edom.
