<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Now the sons of Issachar were, Tola, and Puah, Jashub, and Shimron, four.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> And the sons of Tola; Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and Jibsam, and Shemuel, heads of their father's house, to wit, of Tola: they were valiant men of might in their generations; whose number was in the days of David two and twenty thousand and six hundred.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> And the sons of Uzzi; Izrahiah: and the sons of Izrahiah; Michael, and Obadiah, and Joel, Ishiah, five: all of them chief men.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And with them, by their generations, after the house of their fathers, were bands of soldiers for war, six and thirty thousand men: for they had many wives and sons.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And their brethren among all the families of Issachar were valiant men of might, reckoned in all by their genealogies fourscore and seven thousand.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> The sons of Benjamin; Bela, and Becher, and Jediael, three.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And the sons of Bela; Ezbon, and Uzzi, and Uzziel, and Jerimoth, and Iri, five; heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of valour; and were reckoned by their genealogies twenty and two thousand and thirty and four.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And the sons of Becher; Zemira, and Joash, and Eliezer, and Elioenai, and Omri, and Jerimoth, and Abiah, and Anathoth, and Alameth. All these are the sons of Becher.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And the number of them, after their genealogy by their generations, heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of valour, was twenty thousand and two hundred.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> The sons also of Jediael; Bilhan: and the sons of Bilhan; Jeush, and Benjamin, and Ehud, and Chenaanah, and Zethan, and Tharshish, and Ahishahar.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> All these the sons of Jediael, by the heads of their fathers, mighty men of valour, were seventeen thousand and two hundred soldiers, fit to go out for war and battle.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Shuppim also, and Huppim, the children of Ir, and Hushim, the sons of Aher.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> The sons of Naphtali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, the sons of Bilhah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> The sons of Manasseh; Ashriel, whom she bare: (but his concubine the Aramitess bare Machir the father of Gilead:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And Machir took to wife the sister of Huppim and Shuppim, whose sister's name was Maachah;) and the name of the second was Zelophehad: and Zelophehad had daughters.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And Maachah the wife of Machir bare a son, and she called his name Peresh; and the name of his brother was Sheresh; and his sons were Ulam and Rakem.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And the sons of Ulam; Bedan. These were the sons of Gilead, the son of Machir, the son of Manasseh.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And his sister Hammoleketh bare Ishod, and Abiezer, and Mahalah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And the sons of Shemida were, Ahian, and Shechem, and Likhi, and Aniam.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And the sons of Ephraim; Shuthelah, and Bered his son, and Tahath his son, and Eladah his son, and Tahath his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And Zabad his son, and Shuthelah his son, and Ezer, and Elead, whom the men of Gath that were born in that land slew, because they came down to take away their cattle.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And Ephraim their father mourned many days, and his brethren came to comfort him.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And when he went in to his wife, she conceived, and bare a son, and he called his name Beriah, because it went evil with his house.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> (And his daughter was Sherah, who built Bethhoron the nether, and the upper, and Uzzensherah.)

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> And Rephah was his son, also Resheph, and Telah his son, and Tahan his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> Laadan his son, Ammihud his son, Elishama his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> Non his son, Jehoshua his son.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> And their possessions and habitations were, Bethel and the towns thereof, and eastward Naaran, and westward Gezer, with the towns thereof; Shechem also and the towns thereof, unto Gaza and the towns thereof:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And by the borders of the children of Manasseh, Bethshean and her towns, Taanach and her towns, Megiddo and her towns, Dor and her towns. In these dwelt the children of Joseph the son of Israel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> The sons of Asher; Imnah, and Isuah, and Ishuai, and Beriah, and Serah their sister.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> And the sons of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel, who is the father of Birzavith.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And Heber begat Japhlet, and Shomer, and Hotham, and Shua their sister.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And the sons of Japhlet; Pasach, and Bimhal, and Ashvath. These are the children of Japhlet.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> And the sons of Shamer; Ahi, and Rohgah, Jehubbah, and Aram.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> And the sons of his brother Helem; Zophah, and Imna, and Shelesh, and Amal.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> The sons of Zophah; Suah, and Harnepher, and Shual, and Beri, and Imrah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> Bezer, and Hod, and Shamma, and Shilshah, and Ithran, and Beera.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>38</bmstyle> And the sons of Jether; Jephunneh, and Pispah, and Ara.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> And the sons of Ulla; Arah, and Haniel, and Rezia.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> All these were the children of Asher, heads of their father's house, choice and mighty men of valour, chief of the princes. And the number throughout the genealogy of them that were apt to the war and to battle was twenty and six thousand men.
