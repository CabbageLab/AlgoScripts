<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And Shaharaim begat children in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> Beriah also, and Shema, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife's name was Maachah:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> And the son of Jonathan was Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat Micah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> And the sons of Micah were, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> And Moza begat Binea: Rapha was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>38</bmstyle> And Azel had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of Benjamin.
