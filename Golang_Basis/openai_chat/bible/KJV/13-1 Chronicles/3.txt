<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> The third, Absalom the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, Adonijah the son of Haggith:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> These six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he reigned seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bathshua the daughter of Ammiel:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> These were all the sons of David, beside the sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And Solomon's son was Rehoboam, Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> Amon his son, Josiah his son.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And the sons of Josiah were, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And the sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And the sons of Pedaiah were, Zerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushabhesed, five.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> And the sons of Elioenai were, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven.
