<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> And if his oblation be a sacrifice of peace offering, if he offer it of the herd; whether it be a male or female, he shall offer it without blemish before the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it at the door of the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron's sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And Aaron's sons shall burn it on the altar upon the burnt sacrifice, which is upon the wood that is on the fire: it is an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> And if his offering for a sacrifice of peace offering unto the LORD be of the flock; male or female, he shall offer it without blemish.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> If he offer a lamb for his offering, then shall he offer it before the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron's sons shall sprinkle the blood thereof round about upon the altar.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat thereof, and the whole rump, it shall he take off hard by the backbone; and the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And the priest shall burn it upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire unto the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And if his offering be a goat, then he shall offer it before the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of it, and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation: and the sons of Aaron shall sprinkle the blood thereof upon the altar round about.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And he shall offer thereof his offering, even an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And the priest shall burn them upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire for a sweet savour: all the fat is the LORD'S.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> It shall be a perpetual statute for your generations throughout all your dwellings, that ye eat neither fat nor blood.
