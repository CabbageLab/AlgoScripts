<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> And the rulers of the people dwelt at Jerusalem: the rest of the people also cast lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in Jerusalem the holy city, and nine parts to dwell in other cities.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> And the people blessed all the men, that willingly offered themselves to dwell at Jerusalem.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Now these are the chief of the province that dwelt in Jerusalem: but in the cities of Judah dwelt every one in his possession in their cities, to wit, Israel, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon's servants.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And at Jerusalem dwelt certain of the children of Judah, and of the children of Benjamin. Of the children of Judah; Athaiah the son of Uzziah, the son of Zechariah, the son of Amariah, the son of Shephatiah, the son of Mahalaleel, of the children of Perez;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And Maaseiah the son of Baruch, the son of Colhozeh, the son of Hazaiah, the son of Adaiah, the son of Joiarib, the son of Zechariah, the son of Shiloni.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> All the sons of Perez that dwelt at Jerusalem were four hundred threescore and eight valiant men.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And these are the sons of Benjamin; Sallu the son of Meshullam, the son of Joed, the son of Pedaiah, the son of Kolaiah, the son of Maaseiah, the son of Ithiel, the son of Jesaiah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And after him Gabbai, Sallai, nine hundred twenty and eight.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And Joel the son of Zichri was their overseer: and Judah the son of Senuah was second over the city.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> Of the priests: Jedaiah the son of Joiarib, Jachin.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> Seraiah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok, the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, was the ruler of the house of God.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And their brethren that did the work of the house were eight hundred twenty and two: and Adaiah the son of Jeroham, the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi, the son of Zechariah, the son of Pashur, the son of Malchiah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And his brethren, chief of the fathers, two hundred forty and two: and Amashai the son of Azareel, the son of Ahasai, the son of Meshillemoth, the son of Immer,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And their brethren, mighty men of valour, an hundred twenty and eight: and their overseer was Zabdiel, the son of one of the great men.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> Also of the Levites: Shemaiah the son of Hashub, the son of Azrikam, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Bunni;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And Shabbethai and Jozabad, of the chief of the Levites, had the oversight of the outward business of the house of God.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And Mattaniah the son of Micha, the son of Zabdi, the son of Asaph, was the principal to begin the thanksgiving in prayer: and Bakbukiah the second among his brethren, and Abda the son of Shammua, the son of Galal, the son of Jeduthun.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> All the Levites in the holy city were two hundred fourscore and four.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> Moreover the porters, Akkub, Talmon, and their brethren that kept the gates, were an hundred seventy and two.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And the residue of Israel, of the priests, and the Levites, were in all the cities of Judah, every one in his inheritance.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> But the Nethinims dwelt in Ophel: and Ziha and Gispa were over the Nethinims.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> The overseer also of the Levites at Jerusalem was Uzzi the son of Bani, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Mattaniah, the son of Micha. Of the sons of Asaph, the singers were over the business of the house of God.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> For it was the king's commandment concerning them, that a certain portion should be for the singers, due for every day.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> And Pethahiah the son of Meshezabeel, of the children of Zerah the son of Judah, was at the king's hand in all matters concerning the people.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> And for the villages, with their fields, some of the children of Judah dwelt at Kirjatharba, and in the villages thereof, and at Dibon, and in the villages thereof, and at Jekabzeel, and in the villages thereof,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> And at Jeshua, and at Moladah, and at Bethphelet,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> And at Hazarshual, and at Beersheba, and in the villages thereof,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> And at Ziklag, and at Mekonah, and in the villages thereof,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And at Enrimmon, and at Zareah, and at Jarmuth,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> Zanoah, Adullam, and in their villages, at Lachish, and the fields thereof, at Azekah, and in the villages thereof. And they dwelt from Beersheba unto the valley of Hinnom.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> The children also of Benjamin from Geba dwelt at Michmash, and Aija, and Bethel, and in their villages,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And at Anathoth, Nob, Ananiah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> Hazor, Ramah, Gittaim,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> Hadid, Zeboim, Neballat,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> Lod, and Ono, the valley of craftsmen.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> And of the Levites were divisions in Judah, and in Benjamin.
