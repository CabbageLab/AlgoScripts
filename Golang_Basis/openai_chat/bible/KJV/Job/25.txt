<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman?

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> Behold even to the moon, and it shineth not; yea, the stars are not pure in his sight.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> How much less man, that is a worm? and the son of man, which is a worm?
