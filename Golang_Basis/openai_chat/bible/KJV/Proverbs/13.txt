<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> A wise son heareth his father's instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth: but the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life: but he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> A righteous man hateth lying: but a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing: there is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> The ransom of a man's life are his riches: but the poor heareth not rebuke.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> The light of the righteous rejoiceth: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> Only by pride cometh contention: but with the well advised is wisdom.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Hope deferred maketh the heart sick: but when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> The law of the wise is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> Good understanding giveth favour: but the way of transgressors is hard.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge: but a fool layeth open his folly.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> A wicked messenger falleth into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul: but it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> He that walketh with wise men shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be destroyed.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> Evil pursueth sinners: but to the righteous good shall be repayed.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> Much food is in the tillage of the poor: but there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> He that spareth his rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.
