<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> He, that being often reproved hardeneth his neck, shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice: but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father: but he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth his substance.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> The king by judgment establisheth the land: but he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> A man that flattereth his neighbour spreadeth a net for his feet.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare: but the righteous doth sing and rejoice.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> The righteous considereth the cause of the poor: but the wicked regardeth not to know it.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> Scornful men bring a city into a snare: but wise men turn away wrath.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> If a wise man contendeth with a foolish man, whether he rage or laugh, there is no rest.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> The bloodthirsty hate the upright: but the just seek his soul.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> A fool uttereth all his mind: but a wise man keepeth it in till afterwards.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> If a ruler hearken to lies, all his servants are wicked.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> The poor and the deceitful man meet together: the LORD lighteneth both their eyes.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, his throne shall be established for ever.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> The rod and reproof give wisdom: but a child left to himself bringeth his mother to shame.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth: but the righteous shall see their fall.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> Correct thy son, and he shall give thee rest; yea, he shall give delight unto thy soul.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> A servant will not be corrected by words: for though he understand he will not answer.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words? there is more hope of a fool than of him.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child shall have him become his son at the length.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> An angry man stirreth up strife, and a furious man aboundeth in transgression.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> A man's pride shall bring him low: but honour shall uphold the humble in spirit.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul: he heareth cursing, and bewrayeth it not.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> The fear of man bringeth a snare: but whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> Many seek the ruler's favour; but every man's judgment cometh from the LORD.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> An unjust man is an abomination to the just: and he that is upright in the way is abomination to the wicked.
