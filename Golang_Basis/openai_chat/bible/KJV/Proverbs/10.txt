<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> He that gathereth in summer is a wise son: but he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> The memory of the just is blessed: but the name of the wicked shall rot.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> The wise in heart will receive commandments: but a prating fool shall fall.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> He that walketh uprightly walketh surely: but he that perverteth his ways shall be known.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow: but a prating fool shall fall.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> Hatred stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> Wise men lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish is near destruction.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> The rich man's wealth is his strong city: the destruction of the poor is their poverty.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> The labour of the righteous tendeth to life: the fruit of the wicked to sin.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction: but he that refuseth reproof erreth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> He that hideth hatred with lying lips, and he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin: but he that refraineth his lips is wise.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> The tongue of the just is as choice silver: the heart of the wicked is little worth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> The lips of the righteous feed many: but fools die for want of wisdom.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> The blessing of the LORD, it maketh rich, and he addeth no sorrow with it.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> It is as sport to a fool to do mischief: but a man of understanding hath wisdom.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him: but the desire of the righteous shall be granted.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more: but the righteous is an everlasting foundation.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, so is the sluggard to them that send him.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> The fear of the LORD prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> The hope of the righteous shall be gladness: but the expectation of the wicked shall perish.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> The way of the LORD is strength to the upright: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> The righteous shall never be removed: but the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> The mouth of the just bringeth forth wisdom: but the froward tongue shall be cut out.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable: but the mouth of the wicked speaketh frowardness.
