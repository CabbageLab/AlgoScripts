<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> The sons of Judah; Pharez, Hezron, and Carmi, and Hur, and Shobal.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> And Reaiah the son of Shobal begat Jahath; and Jahath begat Ahumai, and Lahad. These are the families of the Zorathites.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> And these were of the father of Etam; Jezreel, and Ishma, and Idbash: and the name of their sister was Hazelelponi:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And Penuel the father of Gedor, and Ezer the father of Hushah. These are the sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah, the father of Bethlehem.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> And Ashur the father of Tekoa had two wives, Helah and Naarah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> And Naarah bare him Ahuzam, and Hepher, and Temeni, and Haahashtari. These were the sons of Naarah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And the sons of Helah were, Zereth, and Jezoar, and Ethnan.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And Coz begat Anub, and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel the son of Harum.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> And Jabez was more honourable than his brethren: and his mother called his name Jabez, saying, Because I bare him with sorrow.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And Jabez called on the God of Israel, saying, Oh that thou wouldest bless me indeed, and enlarge my coast, and that thine hand might be with me, and that thou wouldest keep me from evil, that it may not grieve me! And God granted him that which he requested.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And Chelub the brother of Shuah begat Mehir, which was the father of Eshton.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And Eshton begat Bethrapha, and Paseah, and Tehinnah the father of Irnahash. These are the men of Rechah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And the sons of Kenaz; Othniel, and Seraiah: and the sons of Othniel; Hathath.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> And Meonothai begat Ophrah: and Seraiah begat Joab, the father of the valley of Charashim; for they were craftsmen.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> And the sons of Caleb the son of Jephunneh; Iru, Elah, and Naam: and the sons of Elah, even Kenaz.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> And the sons of Jehaleleel; Ziph, and Ziphah, Tiria, and Asareel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And the sons of Ezra were, Jether, and Mered, and Epher, and Jalon: and she bare Miriam, and Shammai, and Ishbah the father of Eshtemoa.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And his wife Jehudijah bare Jered the father of Gedor, and Heber the father of Socho, and Jekuthiel the father of Zanoah. And these are the sons of Bithiah the daughter of Pharaoh, which Mered took.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And the sons of his wife Hodiah the sister of Naham, the father of Keilah the Garmite, and Eshtemoa the Maachathite.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And the sons of Shimon were, Amnon, and Rinnah, Benhanan, and Tilon. And the sons of Ishi were, Zoheth, and Benzoheth.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> The sons of Shelah the son of Judah were, Er the father of Lecah, and Laadah the father of Mareshah, and the families of the house of them that wrought fine linen, of the house of Ashbea,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And Jokim, and the men of Chozeba, and Joash, and Saraph, who had the dominion in Moab, and Jashubilehem. And these are ancient things.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> These were the potters, and those that dwelt among plants and hedges: there they dwelt with the king for his work.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> The sons of Simeon were, Nemuel, and Jamin, Jarib, Zerah, and Shaul:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> Shallum his son, Mibsam his son, Mishma his son.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> And the sons of Mishma; Hamuel his son, Zacchur his son, Shimei his son.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> And Shimei had sixteen sons and six daughters; but his brethren had not many children, neither did all their family multiply, like to the children of Judah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> And they dwelt at Beersheba, and Moladah, and Hazarshual,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And at Bilhah, and at Ezem, and at Tolad,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> And at Bethuel, and at Hormah, and at Ziklag,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> And at Bethmarcaboth, and Hazarsusim, and at Bethbirei, and at Shaaraim. These were their cities unto the reign of David.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And their villages were, Etam, and Ain, Rimmon, and Tochen, and Ashan, five cities:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And all their villages that were round about the same cities, unto Baal. These were their habitations, and their genealogy.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> And Meshobab, and Jamlech, and Joshah the son of Amaziah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> And Joel, and Jehu the son of Josibiah, the son of Seraiah, the son of Asiel,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> And Elioenai, and Jaakobah, and Jeshohaiah, and Asaiah, and Adiel, and Jesimiel, and Benaiah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> And Ziza the son of Shiphi, the son of Allon, the son of Jedaiah, the son of Shimri, the son of Shemaiah;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>38</bmstyle> These mentioned by their names were princes in their families: and the house of their fathers increased greatly.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> And they went to the entrance of Gedor, even unto the east side of the valley, to seek pasture for their flocks.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> And they found fat pasture and good, and the land was wide, and quiet, and peaceable; for they of Ham had dwelt there of old.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>41</bmstyle> And these written by name came in the days of Hezekiah king of Judah, and smote their tents, and the habitations that were found there, and destroyed them utterly unto this day, and dwelt in their rooms: because there was pasture there for their flocks.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>42</bmstyle> And some of them, even of the sons of Simeon, five hundred men, went to mount Seir, having for their captains Pelatiah, and Neariah, and Rephaiah, and Uzziel, the sons of Ishi.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>43</bmstyle> And they smote the rest of the Amalekites that were escaped, and dwelt there unto this day.
