<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>1</bmstyle> These are the sons of Israel; Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>2</bmstyle> Dan, Joseph, and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>3</bmstyle> The sons of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah: which three were born unto him of the daughter of Shua the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of Judah, was evil in the sight of the LORD; and he slew him.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>4</bmstyle> And Tamar his daughter in law bare him Pharez and Zerah. All the sons of Judah were five.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>5</bmstyle> The sons of Pharez; Hezron, and Hamul.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>6</bmstyle> And the sons of Zerah; Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara: five of them in all.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>7</bmstyle> And the sons of Carmi; Achar, the troubler of Israel, who transgressed in the thing accursed.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>8</bmstyle> And the sons of Ethan; Azariah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>9</bmstyle> The sons also of Hezron, that were born unto him; Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>10</bmstyle> And Ram begat Amminadab; and Amminadab begat Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah;

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>11</bmstyle> And Nahshon begat Salma, and Salma begat Boaz,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>12</bmstyle> And Boaz begat Obed, and Obed begat Jesse,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>13</bmstyle> And Jesse begat his firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimma the third,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>14</bmstyle> Nethaneel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>15</bmstyle> Ozem the sixth, David the seventh:

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>16</bmstyle> Whose sisters were Zeruiah, and Abigail. And the sons of Zeruiah; Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>17</bmstyle> And Abigail bare Amasa: and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmeelite.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>18</bmstyle> And Caleb the son of Hezron begat children of Azubah his wife, and of Jerioth: her sons are these; Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>19</bmstyle> And when Azubah was dead, Caleb took unto him Ephrath, which bare him Hur.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>20</bmstyle> And Hur begat Uri, and Uri begat Bezaleel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>21</bmstyle> And afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he married when he was threescore years old; and she bare him Segub.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>22</bmstyle> And Segub begat Jair, who had three and twenty cities in the land of Gilead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>23</bmstyle> And he took Geshur, and Aram, with the towns of Jair, from them, with Kenath, and the towns thereof, even threescore cities. All these belonged to the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>24</bmstyle> And after that Hezron was dead in Calebephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the father of Tekoa.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>25</bmstyle> And the sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were, Ram the firstborn, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, and Ahijah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>26</bmstyle> Jerahmeel had also another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>27</bmstyle> And the sons of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were, Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>28</bmstyle> And the sons of Onam were, Shammai, and Jada. And the sons of Shammai; Nadab, and Abishur.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>29</bmstyle> And the name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail, and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>30</bmstyle> And the sons of Nadab; Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>31</bmstyle> And the sons of Appaim; Ishi. And the sons of Ishi; Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan; Ahlai.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>32</bmstyle> And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai; Jether, and Jonathan: and Jether died without children.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>33</bmstyle> And the sons of Jonathan; Peleth, and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>34</bmstyle> Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>35</bmstyle> And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>36</bmstyle> And Attai begat Nathan, and Nathan begat Zabad,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>37</bmstyle> And Zabad begat Ephlal, and Ephlal begat Obed,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>38</bmstyle> And Obed begat Jehu, and Jehu begat Azariah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>39</bmstyle> And Azariah begat Helez, and Helez begat Eleasah,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>40</bmstyle> And Eleasah begat Sisamai, and Sisamai begat Shallum,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>41</bmstyle> And Shallum begat Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat Elishama.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>42</bmstyle> Now the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were, Mesha his firstborn, which was the father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>43</bmstyle> And the sons of Hebron; Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>44</bmstyle> And Shema begat Raham, the father of Jorkoam: and Rekem begat Shammai.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>45</bmstyle> And the son of Shammai was Maon: and Maon was the father of Bethzur.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>46</bmstyle> And Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bare Haran, and Moza, and Gazez: and Haran begat Gazez.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>47</bmstyle> And the sons of Jahdai; Regem, and Jotham, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>48</bmstyle> Maachah, Caleb's concubine, bare Sheber, and Tirhanah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>49</bmstyle> She bare also Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbenah, and the father of Gibea: and the daughter of Caleb was Achsah.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>50</bmstyle> These were the sons of Caleb the son of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah; Shobal the father of Kirjathjearim,

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>51</bmstyle> Salma the father of Bethlehem, Hareph the father of Bethgader.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>52</bmstyle> And Shobal the father of Kirjathjearim had sons; Haroeh, and half of the Manahethites.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>53</bmstyle> And the families of Kirjathjearim; the Ithrites, and the Puhites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zareathites, and the Eshtaulites.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>54</bmstyle> The sons of Salma; Bethlehem, and the Netophathites, Ataroth, the house of Joab, and half of the Manahethites, the Zorites.

<bmstyle color=#ADADAD font-size=14>55</bmstyle> And the families of the scribes which dwelt at Jabez; the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, and Suchathites. These are the Kenites that came of Hemath, the father of the house of Rechab.
